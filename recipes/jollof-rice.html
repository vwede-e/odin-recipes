Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof Rice is probably the most famous West African dish.

Itâ€™s long-grain rice spiced up with tomatoes, onions, bell pepper, garlic, and thyme, then simmered
in a delicious chicken broth for a flavor-packed comfort food guaranteed to soothe your soul.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes and pepper
  • Seasonings like bay leaf and thyme
  • Chicken broth

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and set it aside. Add tomato, bell pepper, half of the onion in a blender, and puree until smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the remaining chopped onions, garlic, and thyme and sautee for about 2-3 minutes until the onion is translucent.
  4. Then add tomato paste and fry for about three more minutes.
  5. Fry the both side for about 3 to 4 minutes each.
  6. Add in pureed tomato mixture and cook while stirring for about 15-20 minutes.
  7. Add washed rice, bay leaf, Scotch bonnet, bouillon powder, chicken broth, and water.
  8. Give everything a generous stir, then season with salt to taste.
  9. Bring the liquids to a rolling boil, then cover and reduce heat to low.
  10. Allow rice to cook until tender and fluffy (about 30-40 minutes)
  11. Turn off heat, allow the rice to cool for about 10 minutes, then fluff rice with a fork.
  12. Serve with grilled chicken or meat.
